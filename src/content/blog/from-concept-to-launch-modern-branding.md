Adorned was an augmented reality mobile game that brought digital experiences into the real world. Digital petting zoos at the park. Virtual parades down the street. The technology was cutting-edge—the branding needed to match.

## Branding for Innovation

When you're pitching to investors, your brand does the talking before you open your mouth. Adorned needed a branding kit that communicated innovation, approachability, and the sheer coolness of their AR platform.

**Modern Meets Approachable**: The challenge was balancing cutting-edge with accessible. The target audience—45% female, 38% ages 16-24—needed to see themselves in the brand, not be intimidated by it.

**Visual Language That Speaks**: Every color, every font, every visual element was chosen to reflect the AR experience: vibrant, dynamic, and impossible to ignore.

**Investor-Ready**: This wasn't just about attracting users—it was about attracting funding. The branding kit needed to work in pitch decks, presentations, and investor meetings.

## The Branding Foundation

A strong brand identity isn't decoration—it's strategy. It's the foundation for every marketing decision, every product choice, every user touchpoint.

For Adorned, we created more than a logo and color palette. We created a visual identity that could grow with the company, adapt to new features, and consistently communicate what makes them special in a crowded mobile gaming market of 209,000+ Americans.

## Why Brand Identity Matters

Whether you're launching a startup, refreshing an existing brand, or pitching to investors, your brand identity is your first impression and your lasting impression. Make it count.
